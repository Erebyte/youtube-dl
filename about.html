youtube-dl GUI is a user interface built on top of the youtube-dl and pafy api. Used to search for and download specific videos off youtube and convert them to mp3 (other formats avalable in future releases). Includes playlist support.

Help:

How to use:

  • Search for video title in search bar.
  • Select your video of choice then press "Download".
  • Your video should download and then be found in the "downloads" folder next to the application.
